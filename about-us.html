About us

Hey there! Welcome to BigFanofMaths.com - We’re a small but mighty team of three: Joeliyn (Jojo), Akshaiya (Akshie) and Bernard (Bernie). We’ve put our heads together to bring you a website that adds up to a whole lot of fun.

We noticed that while there are plenty of maths resources for GCSE (KS4) and A-level (KS5) students, Year 7 and Year 8 (KS3) often get left out of the equation. So, we decided to use our passion for maths to create a space where younger students can sharpen their skills with daily questions—and maybe even find out that maths isn’t as acute as it seems!

Jojo and Akshie are the tech wizards here. Fresh out of their A-levels, they built this website from the ground up and keep everything running smoothly. Their determination and love for learning are what make this whole thing possible! Bernie’s our go-to guy for the daily questions. With years of experience as a secondary school maths teacher, he knows just how important it is to nail the basics before moving on to the tricky stuff.

But here’s where we get to the fun part—every day, along with your maths questions, we throw in an EPIC maths joke to keep the students smiling. After all, why should numbers be the only things that multiply? We want the laughs to add up too!

What’s our goal? We want to help younger students get into the habit of tackling maths questions every day. In the future, we would like to work on a tracking system so you can see which topics you shine and which ones you might need a bit more practice. We’re here to show that maths can be just as fun as it is important. If you have any feedback or ideas about how we can improve the website, we would be eager to know!
